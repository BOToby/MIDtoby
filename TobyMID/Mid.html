  Counter Strike:Global Offensive is a first person shooting game jointly developed by valve and hidden path entertainment, which was officially launched in Europe and the United States on August 21, 2012. The game players are divided into two teams: the Counter-terrorit(CT)and the terrorist(T). Both sides need to fight for multiple rounds on one map. If they reach the goal required by the map or eliminate all enemies, they will win.
